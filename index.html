"Coffee: because adulting is hard without a constant caffeine infusion."

Coffee contains caffeine, a natural stimulant that can temporarily ward off drowsiness and restore alertness. Coffee is a popular beverage made from roasted coffee beans, which are the seeds of the Coffea plant. The beans are typically extracted from the fruit, dried, and then roasted to varying degrees, depending on the desired flavor profile.
